Support

We’re here to help.

Quillory is designed to be simple, calm, and private. If something feels unclear or isn’t working as expected, you can reach us anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your recordings and summaries are private by default. We don’t sell your data or use it for advertising. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

Who can access my recordings?

Only you. Your content is stored securely and is not shared with other users.

Can I delete my recordings or summaries?

Yes. You can delete individual recordings, summaries, or your account at any time from within the app.

Does Quillory give medical or mental health advice?

No. Quillory is a personal reflection tool only. Any summaries or insights are not medical, legal, or professional advice.

Is Quillory free?

Quillory may offer free and paid features. Any pricing or subscription details will be clearly shown inside the app.

I found a bug or something isn’t working

Please email us with a brief description of the issue and your device type. This helps us fix things quickly.
