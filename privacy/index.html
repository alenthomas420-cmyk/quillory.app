Privacy Policy

Effective date: YYYY-MM-DD

Quillory is a private voice journaling app. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • Account info: email and basic account identifiers.
  • Your content: voice recordings, transcripts, summaries, and any photos you attach (if you use these features).
  • Usage data: basic app diagnostics (e.g., crash logs) to keep the app stable.

2) How we use information

  • Provide the app (save memories, generate summaries).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • Improve reliability (bug fixes, performance).

3) Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help run the app (hosting, analytics, email support) only as needed.

4) Data retention

You can delete content within the app. We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

5) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6) Your rights (Canada)

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at support@quillory.app.
